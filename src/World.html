Welcome to Turtle Bob's Cave!

Help Bob explore his world and get ready for the season!

How to Play:

  • Move Your View: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move forward, left, backward, and right. Use Q and E to turn left and right, or use your mouse to look around.
  • Build with Algae: Press F to add a block of algae, and G to remove one. See what you can create!
  • Pebble Hunt Game: Bob needs to collect a specific number of pebbles. Click "Start Pebble Hunt!" and try to guess how many pebbles are hidden in the world to help him out.

Go ahead, explore the world, build cool algae structures, and help Bob with his pebble collection!
